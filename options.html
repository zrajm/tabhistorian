Tab Historian

This Chrome extension will let you restore tabs and their history.

The extension keeps track of your browsing, and is then able to restore tabs (complete with history!) on command. The tabs can be kept around for as long as you wish, and are not arbitrarily forgotten (like the ‘Recent tabs’ are).

Since the Google Chrome API does not give access to each tab’s past history, this extension remember history that has occurred since it was installed. Browsing history that takes place while the extension is off is not registered.
